Fun Facts

  • They are one of three species not protected federally, along with the European Starling and Rock Pigeion. This is due to them being invasive species.
  • They can swim if needs must, though are never observed swimming for any purpose other than to escape water.
  • They are able to live in many environments, from snowy New York streets to the arid Sonoran Desert.
  • They can live up to 13 years.
